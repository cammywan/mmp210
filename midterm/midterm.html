Midterm -- Spring Break By the Beach
Cammy Wan

My theme is spring break by the beach. I divided the canvas in three equal parts,
the top is clouds which changes color and sizes whenever you refresh the page, and I used three ellipse to create each cloud.
The middle part is waves, it changes color and size as well, for this I used several arcs to creat each wave pattern.
The bottom part is the beach or the sand, light yellow color represents the sand and it changes color.
